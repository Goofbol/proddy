# Contributing to Proddy Agent

Proddy Agent is a design repo first and a codebase second. The most useful contributions right now are ideas, critiques, and patterns — not just pull requests.

## How to contribute

- **Open an issue before a PR.** Describe the use case or the problem first. A short conversation saves a rejected PR.
- **Ideas count.** A clear write-up of a workflow Proddy should handle, or a failure mode it should guard against, is as valuable as code.
- **Keep changes small and traceable.** One concern per PR. Explain what it does and why in the description.
- **Match the voice.** Docs and prompts here read like a person wrote them — plain, direct, no filler. Hold your contribution to the same bar.

## Clean-room rule

Do not paste proprietary or employer-confidential material into this repo — internal prompts, private agent configs, client names, tenant identifiers, or anything you don't have the right to publish. Contribute the *idea*, described from scratch. If you wouldn't put it on your own public profile, it doesn't belong here.

This project is licensed under the [MIT License](LICENSE). By submitting a contribution, you agree that your work is your own to give and that it is licensed under the same MIT terms — inbound contributions are accepted under the same license as the outbound project. No separate CLA.

## Reporting something sensitive

If you spot leaked credentials, personal data, or anything that shouldn't be public, open an issue without quoting the sensitive value, or reach out privately through the profile at [github.com/Goofbol](https://github.com/Goofbol).
